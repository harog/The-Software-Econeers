Breakfast Recipe

Bacon and Eggs

Ingredients

  • ethical dilemma
  • eggs
  • milk or milk substitute
  • chedder cheese
  • chives

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick pan on medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil and desired amount of bacon, tofurkey, or plant-based sausage. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook until desired texture has been achieved.
  2. After cooked, cut protein into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat and add a tablespoon of butter. Meanwhile, whisk eggs with a splash of milk or milk substitute. Add desired amount of chives and cheese. Mix in protein. Cook in pan, stirring occassionally until desired texture has been achieved.
  4. Plate scramlbe. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add hot sauce if desired. Enjoy!
