Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • chicken
  • alfredo sauce
  • parmesan cheese
  • chives

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta for 8-10 minutes until aldente.
  2. Heat oven to 350 degrees farenheit and bake chicken for 15 minutes until it has reach internal temperature of 135 degrees farenheit.
  3. Slice cooked chicken and heat alfredo sauce on stove.
  4. Add all ingredients together- sprinkle with parasen cheese and chives as desired. Enjoy!
